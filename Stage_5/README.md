# Project Closure Report

## Results Summary

Rafeeq is a digital platform designed to connect elderly individuals and their families with verified companions who can assist them during daily activities and essential outings. The platform aims to provide a safe, reliable, and convenient way to request companion services while ensuring transparency and ease of communication.

The MVP successfully delivered the core functionalities defined in the Project Charter, including:

* Companion booking and request management.
* Secure online payment integration.
* Real-time chat between clients and companions.
* Live trip tracking using GPS.
* Trip status management (accept, start, and complete trips).
* Rating and feedback system.
* Support ticket submission.
* Role-based access for clients, companions, and administrators.

The project achieved most of its planned objectives and successfully demonstrated the main user journey from requesting a companion to completing and evaluating a trip.

---

## Lessons Learned

### What Went Well

* Team communication remained effective throughout the project.
* Tasks were distributed according to each member’s skills and strengths.
* Early design activities helped establish a clear vision for the platform.
* Conducting user research and visiting an elderly care home provided valuable insights into the needs and challenges faced by our target audience.
* Collaboration among team members helped resolve issues efficiently and maintain project progress.

### Challenges

* Integrating the frontend, backend, and external services required additional effort and coordination.
* Time constraints limited the amount of testing and refinement that could be completed.
* Designing an interface suitable for elderly users required continuous adjustments and simplification.
* Managing multiple technologies and third-party services increased project complexity.

### How We Addressed Them

* Conducted regular team meetings to track progress and discuss blockers.
* Performed technical research to identify appropriate solutions.
* Sought feedback and guidance from mentors.
* Worked collaboratively to troubleshoot and resolve integration issues.

### Future Improvements

* Begin testing earlier in the development cycle.
* Allocate more time for integration testing and bug fixing.
* Improve task tracking and project planning processes.
* Expand the platform with additional services and enhanced companion verification features.
* Develop a dedicated mobile application to improve accessibility and user experience.

---


## Team Retrospective Highlights

The team demonstrated strong collaboration and maintained consistent communication throughout the project. Regular meetings helped ensure alignment on project goals and allowed challenges to be identified and addressed early.

One of the primary challenges involved coordinating development tasks that depended on multiple system components, particularly during frontend-backend integration. Despite these challenges, the team worked effectively together and successfully delivered a functional MVP.

For future projects, the team would benefit from earlier integration testing, more detailed sprint planning, and additional time allocated for quality assurance and final validation activities.
